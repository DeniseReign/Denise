STAND ALONE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL-TIS

        


DESCRIPTION:

Stand Alone Senior High School(SASHS) in Taguig City is a public school that offers a Senior High School program for students in Grades 11 & 12. The school provides various academic strands,including: ABM,HUMMS,STEM,GAS & TVL. The school aims to provide quality education and preapre students for college or the workface. It is located within the Taguig Integrated School and has its own set of programs and activities for students. The school follows certain rules and regulations to maintain order and discipline, and students are expected to attend classes and participate in school activities regularly and actively.

Stand Alone Senior High School(SASHS) within Taguig Integrated School started classes for the school year 2025-2026 on June 16,2025. However,the establishment of SASHS is rooted in the Department of Education's(DepEd) implementation of the Senior High School program, which began in 2016 for Grade 11 and 2017 for Grade 12, as outlined in DepEd Order No,51.s.2015, issued on October 29,2015. This order provided guidelines for implementing the SHS program is existing public junior high schools and integrated schools, as well as establishing stand-alone public SHSs.'
